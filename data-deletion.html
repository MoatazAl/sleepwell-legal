Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from SleepWell, you can do so in the following ways:

  • 1. Open the SleepWell app.
  • 2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account (feature coming soon).
  • 3. Alternatively, you can send an email to sleepwell-support@example.com with the subject Delete My Data.

Upon receiving your request, we will delete your data from our servers within 7 days.
